# Quizzone
Quizzone is a hub of Quizzes from different knowledge sectors , subjects and Disciplines . It is a platform where you can test your knowledge , skills and experience in different sectors . It features MCQ Quizes from General Knowledge , Science , Social Science , Culture &amp; Arts , CBT , Coding , Programming , Markup , Scripting Languages , IT Sectors , Technology , Latest Trends in AI & Core concept of ML etc . <br>
